Dining · Kandalama

Food that tastes
like home

Our Kitchen

Cooked from the land,
served with love

Meals at Galpothuyaya are prepared fresh, on request, using ingredients sourced from the surrounding land and local markets. Our cook brings generations of Sri Lankan culinary tradition to every plate — unhurried, generous, and unmistakably real. All breakfasts are complimentary for guests.

Menu Highlights

What's on the table

Daily Special

Rice & Curry

Multiple curries, dhal, sambol, papadum, and pol roti — a traditional Sri Lankan meal served at lunch and dinner.

Breakfast

Hoppers & String Hoppers

Classic Sri Lankan breakfast made to order each morning — bowl-shaped rice pancakes served with coconut sambol and kiri hodi.

Breakfast

Fresh Tropical Fruit

Seasonal fruits from local farms — papaya, jackfruit, mango, rambutan, and woodapple served fresh each morning.

Evening

Clay Pot & BBQ Nights

On request — outdoor barbecue or traditional clay pot meals cooked over firewood, enjoyed under the stars by the lake.

All Day

Fresh Ginger Tea & King Coconut

Cool down with a fresh king coconut or revive with our spiced ginger tea — available throughout the day at the lodge.

On Request

Vegetarian & Vegan Options

All meals can be tailored to dietary needs. Sri Lankan cuisine is naturally rich in plant-based dishes — just let us know.

The Way We Cook

Slow food in
a fast world

Nothing here is rushed. Our kitchen doesn't have a fixed menu or set hours. You tell us what you're hungry for and when, and we cook it with care. Ingredients come from our own garden, nearby farms, and the morning market in Dambulla — wherever the season takes us.

  • ✦ Made to order — no buffets, no reheated meals
  • ✦ Locally sourced ingredients
  • ✦ Free breakfast for all guests
  • ✦ Dietary needs catered to on request

✦ Included with Every Stay

Free breakfast, every morning

All room types include a complimentary Sri Lankan breakfast — typically hoppers or string hoppers, fresh fruit, tea or coffee, and coconut sambol. Lunch and dinner are available on request at additional cost.
