About the Lodge

A retreat shaped
by nature's hand

Galpothuyaya Eco Lodge sits quietly on the banks of the historic Kandalama Tank, surrounded by native farmland, ancient jungle, and open skies. Here, the tempo of life slows to the rhythm of birdsong, rustling reeds, and the gentle lapping of still water. This is not luxury removed from the earth вЂ” it is luxury rooted in it.

Lakefront on Kandalama Tank with direct water access

Free breakfast, 24-hr room service & curated excursions

Authentic Sri Lankan cuisine from locally sourced ingredients
