The Cultural Triangle

Ancient wonders
on your doorstep

A Hub for Discovery

Galpothuyaya is ideally positioned for those looking to explore Sri Lanka's rich history. Within a short drive, you can climb the fortress of Sigiriya, meditate in the ancient caves of Dambulla, or wander through the medieval ruins of Polonnaruwa. We can arrange transport, guides, and packed lunches for all your excursions.

10–15 km

Sigiriya Rock Fortress

The iconic UNESCO World Heritage Site. Ascend the ancient rock citadel rising 200m above the jungle plain to discover water gardens, mirror walls, and frescoes painted over 1,500 years ago.

✦ Best visited at 7:00 AM to avoid the heat

15 min drive

Dambulla Cave Temple

The largest and best-preserved cave temple complex in Sri Lanka. House to 157 statues of Lord Buddha and sprawling 2,000-year-old murals painted across the natural rock ceiling.

✦ Requires knees and shoulders to be covered

5 min walk

Kandalama Lake

Designed by King Vasabha in the 1st century BC, this ancient reservoir is now a thriving wetland ecosystem. Step through the jungle from our lodge to reach the tranquil shoreline.

✦ Perfect for sunset walks and birdwatching

16 km

Pidurangala Rock

A more rugged hike alternative to Sigiriya. The summit offers breathtaking 360-degree panoramic views over the jungle canopy and a direct, eye-level view of Sigiriya Rock itself.

✦ Highly recommended for sunrise

1.5 hr drive

Polonnaruwa Ancient City

Explore the medieval capital of Sri Lanka. Rent a bicycle to ride among the vast ruins of grand palaces, imposing stupas, and intricately carved moonstone and guardstone gates.

✦ Makes for a perfect day trip

30–45 min drive

Minneriya / Kaudulla Safari

Famous for 'The Gathering' during the dry season, where hundreds of wild Asian elephants congregate around the reservoirs. We can organize a private 4x4 jeep safari directly from the lodge.

✦ Late afternoon is the best time to see elephants

Around the Lodge

Slow down and
look closely

You don't need to travel far to experience the magic of the dry zone. The area immediately surrounding the lodge is teeming with life and quiet beauty, perfect for unhurried days.

Agro-Tourism & Organic Farming

Join our team in the organic farm for hands-on farming demonstrations and harvest activities that supply our farm-to-table cuisine.

Cycling & Nature Trails

Grab a bicycle and explore dedicated cycling trails running through rural villages, or hike our tranquil on-site footpaths.

Camping & Outdoor Recreation

Spend a night closer to the stars with our curated outdoor camping packages, or enjoy jogging and recreational games in our activity zone.

Bird & Wildlife Watching

Over 100 species of birds, toque macaques, and roaming buffalo herds can be seen right from your balcony or the lake shore.

Getting Here

Galpothuyaya is located in the rural Kap Ela village, on the banks of Kandalama Lake. We are approximately 4 hours from Colombo International Airport (CMB).

🚗
Private Transfer

We can arrange a comfortable, air-conditioned vehicle to meet you at the airport or your previous destination.

✈️
Domestic Flights

Cinnamon Air operates daily flights to Sigiriya Domestic Airport (10km from the lodge). We provide complimentary pickup.

🛺

Day-to-day transport around the Cultural Triangle can be arranged with our trusted local drivers and guides.
